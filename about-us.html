Our Story

InkSnap was founded by Oladipo Azeez Olaniyi (A Professional Graphic Designer, Web Designer, Publisher & Consultant), who aims to help businesses and individuals create a strong and enduring brand presence.

With a passion for design, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence, Oladipo embarked on this journey and continually adapts to the evolving printing and branding industry. He has a primary goal of assisting businesses and individuals in establishing a robust and long-lasting brand presence.

Driven by a passion for design, a keen eye for detail, and a dedication to providing top-notch services, Oladipo commenced this venture. Over the years, he has evolved and expanded, consistently adapting to the dynamic nature of the printing and branding industry.

2017: InkSnap launches in Lagos
2018: Apparel & large-format printing added
2021: Automation + nationwide delivery
2025: 2M+ products printed
