Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What are your turnaround times?

Standard orders are completed within 3-5 business days. Rush orders can be completed within 24-48 hours for an additional fee.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our in-house design team can help create professional designs for your printing needs. Contact us for pricing.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, JPG, and PNG files. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDF files.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes, we deliver to all states in Nigeria. Delivery times and costs vary by location.
